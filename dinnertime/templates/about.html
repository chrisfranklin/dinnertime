About Us

We connect hungry people with happy cooks. You may be friends, you may even be housemates or you could be total strangers.

We think that food tastes better when it is shared so we have built this site to help you to get together with one another and share a meal.
So why not signup and arrange a meal for you and your friends, we can import your contacts and message your friends with all the information they need.

Host

Create a meal quickly and easily, import your contacts from Facebook, Twitter, GMail, Yahoo with more coming soon! Let them know when and where, they will be notified via their preferred communication method, we even help with directions and recipes.

Share

We know hosting can be a lot of work for one person and that's why we provide tools to let everyone pitch in. Your meal is arranged around 'Have's 'Need's and 'Want's. Pick a recipe, tell us what you have in the cupboard and we will help your guests bring the neccesary ingredients.

Privacy

We never share your private information with anyone without your explicit consent, if you are meeting people through the internet there is an inherent risk involved. It is up to you to take reasonable measures to ensure your own safety. Never meet strangers alone.
